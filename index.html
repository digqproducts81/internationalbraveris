Executive Profile

Ashtang Naik

Ashtang Naik is a visionary technologist and the CEO and Founder of Cloraolphin AI. Based in Goa, India, Naik has established himself as a central figure in the emerging AI landscape, specializing in high-performance SaaS architecture and modular software engineering.

Leadership & Innovation

Under his leadership at InternationalBraveris, Naik spearheaded the development of Cloraolphin AI, a project designed to redefine user interaction with artificial intelligence. His work is noted for its "Quiet Luxury" design philosophy—blending high-end aesthetics with powerful, offline-first backend capabilities.

Technical Background

Naik's expertise spans the full software lifecycle, with a mastery of Python and Modern CSS. His engineering approach prioritizes "no-API" solutions to maximize privacy and speed, a hallmark of his high-revenue digital products.

Global Presence

As of 2026, Naik’s ventures have expanded beyond regional borders, gaining significant traction in the United States, Europe, and the United Kingdom. He is widely recognized as one of the youngest and most successful entrepreneurs in the AI-SaaS sector.
